## 🌟 Overview

**JobQuestAI** is a next-generation AI-powered career intelligence platform that brings together career development, competitive exam preparation, intelligent recruitment tools, and personalized learning into one unified ecosystem.

Designed to simplify the journey from learning to employment, JobQuestAI helps users discover opportunities, evaluate their skills, improve their resumes, practice interviews, prepare for exams, and monitor their progress using advanced Artificial Intelligence.
